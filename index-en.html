Case Study

Liechtenstein foundation with downstream holding

16–24 weeks · cross-border · fully implemented

A second-generation entrepreneurial family with assets in operating companies (DACH), real estate and liquid assets. Objective: long-term wealth preservation, clear family governance, asset protection and a tax-robust structure for the generational transition.

Reference project

Analysis and target structure

Mapping of family structure, beneficiary class and distribution logic. Tax and substance review (DE / CH / FL). Target structure: Liechtenstein foundation with external foundation council and downstream FL-GmbH as holding company for the transferred corporate group and future investments.

Structure and legal design

Drafting of foundation statutes and beneficiary regulations. Design of protector and advisory board roles. Detailed transaction roadmap and coordination of tax experts in Germany and Liechtenstein.

Tax analysis & tax rulings

In-depth cross-border tax analysis. Securing binding tax rulings in Liechtenstein. Ensuring a tax-optimal migration of investments into the holding company.

Implementation & closing

Coordination of legal advisors in Germany and Liechtenstein. Full documentation preparation. Signing and closing oversight – end to end.

Ongoing governance & board mandate

Ongoing oversight of the foundation structure. Board mandates within the foundation council and the holding company. Reporting, compliance, annual governance review.
